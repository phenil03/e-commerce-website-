## 🎯 Key Design Features

### Background
- **Type**: Diagonal gradient (135deg)
- **Colors**: 4-color smooth transition (#00171F → #003459 → #007EA7 → #00A8E8)
- **Effect**: Modern, professional, eye-catching

### Form Box
- **Background**: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- **Border Radius**: 15px (modern rounded corners)
- **Shadow**: `0 10px 30px rgba(0, 23, 31, 0.3)` (elevation effect)
- **Effect**: Floating, premium appearance

### Input Fields
- **Border**: 2px solid teal blue (#007EA7)
- **Text Color**: Dark navy (#00171F)
- **Focus Border**: Bright cyan (#00A8E8)
- **Focus Background**: Light blue (#f0f8ff)
- **Effect**: Clear visual feedback, excellent readability

### Buttons
- **Style**: Gradient background
- **Default**: #007EA7 → #00A8E8 (left to right)
- **Hover**: #00A8E8 → #007EA7 (reversed) + scale(1.05)
- **Text**: White on gradient
- **Effect**: Interactive, modern, responsive

### Text Elements
- **Headings**: Dark navy (#00171F)
- **Helper Text**: Dark blue (#003459)
- **All text**: High contrast, easily readable
